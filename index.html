Kashmir

Kashmir is a beautiful place in India. It's in the northern part, surrounded by mountains. People there speak different languages, and it has been a place of conflict for a long time. Some want it to be part of India, while others want it to be independent or part of Pakistan. The region has a rich culture, with delicious food and **beautiful scenery**, but the conflict has made life difficult for many people who live there.
